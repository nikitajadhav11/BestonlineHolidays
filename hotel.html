About This Property

Via Mentana 28, 22100, Como Italy
This pleasant hotel is located on the outskirts of Como at some 2 km from the historical centre of the city. The lakeside promenade can be reached within a short 5-minute stroll and the beach and bathing areas are a 20-minute walk away. Guests who prefer the convenience of onsite facilities can opt for a refreshing dip in the roof-top pool and work on that healthy tan on the sun loungers around it while sipping refreshing cocktails from the bar. For excellent dining they should simply visit the terrace grill and savour its delicious meat and seafood specialities. Those who want to try the typical local tastes can head to the ground floor full-service restaurant. Links to the public transport network are available within a kilometre and guests travelling with their private vehicles can use the car park and the garage facilities.
